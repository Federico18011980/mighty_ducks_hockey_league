Sportmanship

The common interest that members of the Association shar is to inspire Youth to practice the ideals of sportmanship and fair play. Any player, coach
team, parent, spectator, administrator or referee whose behavior detracts from
this purpose is subjets to disciplinary action regardless of technical hockey background, expertise, accomplishments or standing.

IHF FIELD REGULATIONS

  • 1. DIMENSIONS.IHF

    Law 1 provides for flexible external field dimensions within a given maximun and minimum width and length. these dimensions should be adhered to for all fields used by teams under 12 and older

  • COMPETITIONS FIELDS.

    Fields used within the competition program must be a minimum of 100 x 60 yards. Leagues, districts or Association participating in the state competition program that are unable to provide a field that meets these minimun requirements must advise the State Competition Board which will assing is Field Comitte to inspect the field and recommend to the Board whether or not a waiver of the minimum dimensions sholuld be granted. Teams from leagues, district or associations that are unable to provide an acceptable field may be requaried to play all their games away
