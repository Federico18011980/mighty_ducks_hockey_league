Sportmanship

The common interest that members of the Association shar is to inspire Youth to practice the ideals of sportmanship and fair play. Any player, coach
team, parent, spectator, administrator or referee whose behavior detracts from
this purpose is subjets to disciplinary action regardless of technical hockey background, expertise, accomplishments or standing.
